In Amateur Radio I am mostly known under the call signs SA5IKN (Sweden), M0SKN (England), US6IKN (Ukraine), and EI6KC (Ireland). I am well-travelled, having visited nearly a hundred countries, of which I operated from over twenty. Throughout the last decade or so you might have worked me as: PJ2/EI6KC, VK/US6IKN, 9M2/M0SKN, EA8/EI6KC, EA6/EI6KC, EO16IKN, EO18IKN, EO20IKN, EI0W (guest contest op), I/, F/, 9A/, YO/, LA/, and over 20 other ... /... or guest calls.

My favourite mode is CW but I enjoy working Digital and SSB too. I am not a big fan of ragchewing and prefer consise and informative QSOs. Chasing DXs and contesting are also among the types of activities I enjoy, however due to job-related constraints it is increasingly difficult for me to allocate time for these. I am active on all bands 160m through 3cm, including Meteor Scatter on UHF and VHF, linear (and rarely FM) LEO satellites, MEO satellite IO-117, GEO satellite QO-100, and EME.
