QSL Cards and LoTW

I promptly upload all my QSOs to LoTW. If you would like to receive a paper QSL card, please refer to the details below. To request my postal address, simply send me an email.

If we had an EME QSO:

I will gladly reply to any paper QSL card confirming an EME contact. No financial contribution is required — just your return address. If I send my QSL card first, I trust the station will kindly return the gesture.

If we had an HF, terrestrial VHF/UHF, or Satellite QSO:

Please use the Club Log OQRS service. Alternatively, you may send your card along with a self-addressed envelope (SAE) and 5 USD. Email me to obtain the correct mailing address.

I am not currently a member of any QSL bureau. Over more than 20 years of amateur radio activity across several countries, I have received and responded to approximately 150 kilograms of bureau QSL cards. While I greatly value the tradition, I now handle all QSL confirmations directly.

I continue to upload my log to eQSL on a regular basis, although this may change in the future. Any electronic "QSL cards" received via email will be treated as spam.
